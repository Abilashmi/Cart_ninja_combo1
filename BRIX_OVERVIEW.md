# Brix — What It Is, Why It Works, and How It Grows Revenue

---

## What is Brix?

**Brix** is the brand behind **The Cart Ninja** — a Shopify app that turns your store's default cart into a revenue-generating sales channel.

Instead of sending customers to a plain cart page, Brix opens a branded slide-out cart drawer loaded with tools that increase what each customer spends before they hit checkout.

### Everything Brix includes:

| Feature | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Cart Drawer** | Replaces the default cart with a fast, branded slide-out drawer |
| **Progress Bar** | Shows customers how close they are to a free shipping goal |
| **Coupon Slider** | Displays clickable discount codes inside the open cart |
| **FBT — Frequently Bought Together** | Shows "customers also bought" upsells inside the cart |
| **Upsell Rules** | Targets product offers based on what's in the cart, cart value, or collections |
| **Combo Forge (Bundle Builder)** | Creates dedicated bundle pages with custom layouts, published to your storefront automatically |
| **AI Assistant (CartNinja)** | Floating chat that configures settings, writes copy, and suggests products |
| **Analytics Dashboard** | Tracks clicks, revenue, and conversions across every Brix feature |

> Brix installs via Shopify's Theme App Extensions — it never touches your theme code and works with all Online Store 2.0 themes (Dawn, Sense, Refresh, Craft, and more).

## Why Choose Brix?

### 1. No Code. No Risk.
Brix uses Shopify's native app embed system. Toggle it on in the Theme Editor — no liquid edits, no developer needed. Switch or update your theme at any time without breaking anything.

### 2. All-in-One vs. Paying for 4–5 Apps
Most stores patch together a cart app, a upsell app, a coupon app, and a bundle app separately. Brix replaces all of them under one subscription, one dashboard, and one support channel.

### 3. Built for the Moment Customers Are Ready to Buy
Every feature fires inside the open cart drawer — the highest-intent moment in the shopping journey. That is when upsells, progress nudges, and discount codes have the biggest impact.

### 4. Live Preview Editor
See exactly how your cart looks as you change colours, fonts, layout, and copy. No save-and-refresh cycle — what you see is what your customers get.

### 5. Smart Upsell Rules
Unlike generic "you might also like" widgets, Brix lets you define rules:
- Show Product B only when Product A is in the cart.
- Show a bundle offer only when the cart is above $30.
- Show collection-based recommendations when a specific category is in the cart.

### 6. AI Built Into Every Workflow
- **CartNinja AI** — a floating assistant on every page that answers questions, suggests settings, and walks you through changes.
- **Brix AI** — inside the Bundle Builder, generates titles, descriptions, and step names for your bundle pages in one click.

### 7. Revenue You Can Actually See
The Analytics Dashboard ties every coupon click, upsell add, and bundle order back to real revenue numbers — so you know which features are earning and which need adjustment.

## Without Brix vs. With Brix — Revenue Comparison

### The Cart Experience

| | Without Brix | With Brix |
|---|---|---|
| **Cart type** | Redirects to `/cart` page — customer leaves the product page | Slide-out drawer opens instantly — customer stays on the page |
| **Impact** | Interrupts momentum; higher chance of bouncing | Frictionless; customer reviews cart and checks out faster |

### Average Order Value (AOV)

| | Without Brix | With Brix |
|---|---|---|
| **Upsells in cart** | None | FBT + Upsell Rules show relevant products while the cart is open |
| **Free shipping nudge** | None | Progress bar: *"You're $12 away from free shipping"* → customer adds more |
| **Bundle offers** | Products sold individually | Combo Forge bundle pages sell 3–5 products together with a discount incentive |
| **Typical AOV lift** | Baseline | **+15% to +25%** when progress bar + FBT are active |

### Coupon & Discount Conversion

| | Without Brix | With Brix |
|---|---|---|
| **Finding a code** | Customer leaves the cart to search for a discount code, often does not return | Coupon Slider shows all available codes inside the open cart — one tap to apply |
| **Impact** | Checkout abandonment from code-hunting | Higher code apply rate; customer stays in the cart and completes the purchase |

### Bundle & Multi-Product Sales

| | Without Brix | With Brix |
|---|---|---|
| **Bundle pages** | Do not exist without custom dev work | Combo Forge creates a live Shopify bundle page in minutes, no code |
| **Layouts** | N/A | Guided Architect, Velocity Stream, Editorial Split — each tested for different product types |
| **Discount incentive** | Manual setup in Shopify each time | Attach a discount code to any bundle inside the builder |

### Data & Optimisation

| | Without Brix | With Brix |
|---|---|---|
| **Cart visibility** | No data on what happens inside the cart | Analytics tracks every coupon click, upsell click, checkout click, and revenue |
| **What you can act on** | Guess what is causing drop-off | See exactly which feature, product, or bundle is driving or losing revenue |

## Revenue Impact — A Simple Example

> A store with **200 orders per month** at an average order value of **$45**:
>
> **Monthly revenue without Brix: $9,000**
>
> After activating Brix (progress bar + FBT + coupon slider):
> - AOV increases from $45 → ~$55 (+$10 from upsell adds and free shipping nudges)
> - Checkout completion improves because customers find and apply codes without leaving the cart
>
> **Monthly revenue with Brix: $11,000**
>
> **That is +$2,000 per month — a 22% revenue lift from the same traffic, with zero increase in ad spend.**

The core insight: **customers who open the cart are already in buying mode.** Brix captures that moment with the right offer, the right nudge, and the right discount — instead of letting them check out with the minimum or abandon entirely.

## Getting Started

1. Install The Cart Ninja from the Shopify App Store.
2. Go to **Online Store → Themes → Customize → App Embeds** → toggle **Cart Ninja ON**.
3. Add the **Cart Drawer block** in the Theme Editor.
4. Customize colours and layout in the **Cart Drawer** editor.
5. Turn on **FBT**, **Progress Bar**, and **Coupon Slider** one by one.
6. Build your first bundle in **Combo Forge**.
7. Check **Analytics** after 7 days to see the impact.

> For help at any step, open the **CartNinja AI assistant** (bottom right of the app dashboard).
